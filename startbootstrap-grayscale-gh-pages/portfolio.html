Echo | Antistrophe-

Genre: Downtempo, Ambient

an·tis·tro·phe: Derivitave of a Greek phrase meaning 'turning back', a rhetorical device that involves the repetition of the same words at the end of consecutive phrases. The repeating vocal sample, drums, and bassline reflect this sentiment.

POLAND. 1948. //HOME-

Genre: Ambient

Liberated from a concentration camp at the end of World War II, a young girl was asked by Polish psychologists to draw her idea of 'home'. Pictured in the album art, this track was made to reflect the chaotic atmosphere it creates.
