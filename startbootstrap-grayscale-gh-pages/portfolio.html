About me

Like a lot of people my age, my interest in technology developed from playing video games growing up. During my junior year of high school I took an AP Computer Science class, and built my first computer.

A lot of my free time that year went into dissecting projects I was working on in class, trying to learn as much as I could. This is when I realized I wanted to work in this field.



Design philosophy

Outside of programming, music has always been my biggest passion in life. To me, Web Design requires the same amount of artistic integrity that my music does, and this is ideally where I would want to start building my career.

My goal in building this portfolio is to build a website that reflects who I am as an individual. The best compliments I've recieved are from friends and family telling me that it "looks like something I would make". This is what I want to bring to a professional enviorment, the ability to connect with people and help them express their vision.







Whether it's in web design, java app development, or something unrelated, these past nine weeks have given me the drive to move into the next chapter of my life. As someone who has struggled with mental health issues in the past, this means more to me than I'm able to put into words. As i move into Eleven Fifty's apprenticeship program, and potentially a career, this site will be updated with new projects I'm working on, as a way to keep relevant people up to date, and as a reminder to myself about where I've come from and what I've accomplished.
